About Me

Hi, I'm Brandon, the owner of Aquaflow Plumbing. I take pride in providing reliable, high-quality residential plumbing services throughout Kitsap, Mason, and Pierce County. My goal in becoming a one-man operation was to offer homeowners fair, honest pricing that larger corporations often can't or won't provide.

When you call Aquaflow, you'll work directly with me, no subcontractors, no middlemen. I'm passionate about my craft, and I treat every home I work in like my own.

While most of my work is local to Kitsap, I'm always willing to travel for the right job if it means helping a homeowner get the reliable service they deserve.

Aquaflow Plumbing is built on hard work, honesty, and strong community connections. While I handle every job personally, I'm fortunate to have an incredible advisor, Brett, whose experience and relationships in the community have helped me reach more homeowners who value dependable, local plumbing service.
